Narrative & Cognitive Web3 Game Studio

About Us

Eversage Lab is an experimental narrative and cognitive game studio, dedicated to underserved audiences — beginning with the 50+ demographic in East Asia. We believe the next evolution of games is not about winning, but about being seen, remembered, and meaningfully challenged.

Our vision is to build games that remember you — games that evolve with your emotional rhythms and cognitive footprints over months and years.

Why Web3?

Traditional game studios optimize for engagement metrics, retention funnels, dopamine loops. We optimize for legacy, identity, and presence — values that require player-owned memories rather than platform-locked data.

  • Accountless Identity (ZK Login)
  • NFT-based Memory Artifacts
  • Tokenized Participation (Time Credit Model)
  • Smart Contract-backed Narrative Branching

Long-Term Vision

We are building a “slow metaverse” anchored not in land or loot, but in memory and meaning. Imagine AI pen pals that evolve with you, story arcs that age alongside you, and memory tokens you can pass to your children.
