About Us

Eversage Lab is an experimental narrative & cognitive game studio focused on memory, meaning, and dignity in play. We build stories that evolve with your emotional rhythms over months and years.

Our starting audience is the 50+ demographic in East Asia. We design for accessibility, warmth, and sustained cognitive challenge — blending UX calmness with playful ritual.

Team's Note — Why this Lab?

After years in digital strategy and cross‑border commerce, I’ve seen how products chase metrics and forget people. Games can be different: slow, caring, and beautifully persistent. This lab is my response — a place to craft memory‑driven experiences that age with you.

Why Web3?

We optimize for legacy, identity, presence — values that require player‑owned memories, not platform‑locked data.

  • Accountless Identity (ZK login) for privacy‑first onboarding.
  • Memory Artifacts (ERC‑721 / 1155) — composable visual/audio diaries.
  • Time Tokens (non‑speculative, soulbound) — used for narrative voting and co‑creation.
  • Smart‑contract branching — micro‑communities influence storylines with on‑chain provenance.

Prototype Stack

  • Frontend: React • Unity WebGL • Three.js
  • Identity: WalletConnect • Lit • ZK proofs
  • Narrative Logic: LLM‑assisted + L2 contracts
  • Storage: IPFS/Arweave + hybrid state
  • Economy: Soulbound time tokens

Design Tenets

  • Dignified onboarding, zero‑pressure loops
  • Readable type, high contrast, calm motion
  • Years‑long arcs, seasonal rituals
  • Community memory without extractive hype
